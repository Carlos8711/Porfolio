From a very young age, I have always felt a deep connection with nature and an insatiable curiosity to understand the wonderful processes that govern the natural world. This passion led me to study zoology and to dedicate my career to research, conservation, and education about our natural environment. Throughout my career, I have had the opportunity to participate in various field research projects, where I have explored [relevant places, e.g., the Amazon rainforest, coral reefs, the Andes mountains].
